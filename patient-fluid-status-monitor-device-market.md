<p><p>The market for Patient Fluid Status Monitor Devices can be categorized into two types: invasive and non-invasive. On the other hand, non-invasive devices utilize external sensors or technologies to monitor fluid status without the need for invasive procedures, offering a safer and less intrusive method of measurement, although with potentially slightly lower accuracy.</p></p>
