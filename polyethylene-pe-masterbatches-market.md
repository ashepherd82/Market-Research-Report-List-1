<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) masterbatches are additives used in the plastic industry to enhance the properties of polyethylene, one of the most widely used plastics. HDPE masterbatches are designed for high-density polyethylene, offering benefits like UV protection and improved strength. LLDPE masterbatches are used for linear low-density polyethylene, providing good processing and optical properties. These masterbatches cater to specific polyethylene types, fulfilling different requirements such as color enhancement, UV protection, and enhanced properties for various applications in industries like packaging, construction, automotive, and more.</p></p>
