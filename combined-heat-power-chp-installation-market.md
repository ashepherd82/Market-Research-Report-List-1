<p><p>Combined Heat & Power (CHP) installation, also known as cogeneration, is a method of generating electricity and thermal energy simultaneously. It involves the production of electricity from the waste heat that is typically discharged during power generation. CHP systems capture and utilize this waste heat to generate steam or hot water that can be used for various industrial processes or heating purposes. This highly efficient process results in substantial energy savings and reduced greenhouse gas emissions.</p><p>The future of the Combined Heat & Power (CHP) Installation Market appears to be promising.
