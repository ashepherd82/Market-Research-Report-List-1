Real-time PCR allows for the real-time monitoring and quantification of DNA amplification. Nested PCR with Melt Curve Analysis amplifies specific DNA regions and analyzes the melting temperature of resulting products. PCR with Low-density Microarray utilizes microarrays containing probes for the simultaneous detection of multiple DNA targets. PCR with Liquid Phase Bead Array employs beads labeled with DNA probes to identify various pathogens. PCR with Electrochemical Detection uses electrochemical signals to detect specific DNA sequences, providing rapid and sensitive diagnostics options.</p></p>
