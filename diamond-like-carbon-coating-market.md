<p><p>Diamond-Like Carbon (DLC) coating is categorized into two main types: Hydrogen Free DLC and Hydrogenated DLC. Hydrogen Free DLC, as the name suggests, is a DLC coating that does not contain hydrogen in its composition. This type of coating offers exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making it suitable for various applications such as cutting tools, automotive parts, and medical instruments. On the other hand, Hydrogenated DLC refers to DLC coatings that contain hydrogen in their composition. This type of DLC coating exhibits increased adhesion and flexibility, making it ideal for applications that require low friction and excellent lubricity, such as bearings and moving mechanical parts.</p></p>
