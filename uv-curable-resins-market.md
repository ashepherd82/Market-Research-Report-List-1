<p><p>UV curable resins are a type of polymer that can be hardened or cured quickly when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. There are two main types of UV curable resins: radical UV-curable resin and cationic UV-curable resin. Radical UV-curable resins use a free radical mechanism to initiate the curing process, while cationic UV-curable resins use a cationic mechanism. These resins find applications in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, 3D printing, and electronics due to their fast curing, high durability, and environmental friendliness.</p></p>
