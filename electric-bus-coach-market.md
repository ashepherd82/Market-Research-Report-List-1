<p><p>The electric bus and coach market consists of three main types: Full Battery Electric Buses (BEB), Hybrid Electric Buses (HEB), and Fuel Cell Electric Buses (FCEB). BEBs are powered solely by a rechargeable battery system, offering zero emissions and a longer driving range. HEBs combine an electric motor with an internal combustion engine, enabling lower emissions and improved fuel efficiency. FCEBs use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, resulting in zero emissions and longer ranges compared to other electric buses. These different types cater to varying needs and environmental goals in the transportation sector.</p></p>
