<p><p>The car tachograph market can be categorized into two types: integrated and portable. </p><p>1. Integrated tachographs are built into the vehicle's dashboard and are permanently installed. They are connected to the vehicle's systems, such as the engine and gearbox, and record data automatically. Integrated tachographs provide accurate and real-time information on the vehicle's speed, distance traveled, and driving time, ensuring compliance with transportation laws and regulations.</p><p>2. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are stand-alone devices that can be easily installed and removed from different vehicles. They are compact and mainly used by drivers who operate multiple vehicles or temporary vehicles. Like integrated tachographs, portable ones also record important data but with the advantage of portability and flexibility for different vehicles.</p></p>
