<p><p>The automotive silicone elastomers market consists of various types of silicone materials used in automotive applications. High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV) elastomers are heat-resistant and commonly used in engine components. Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV) elastomers offer easy processing and are used for gaskets and seals. Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is a liquid form of silicone rubber used for complex designs. Fluorosilicone Rubber (FSR) offers excellent fuel and oil resistance. High Consistency Silicone Rubber (HCR) is used for extruded profiles and hoses. Silicone gels are used in electronic sealing applications.</p></p>
