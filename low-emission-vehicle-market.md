<p><p>The Low Emission Vehicle market consists of various types of vehicles. Mild Hybrid Electric Vehicles (MHEVs) combine a conventional internal combustion engine with a small electric motor, assisting in propulsion. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) use a combination of an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, which can operate independently or together. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) have larger battery packs and can be charged from an external power source. Pure Electric Vehicles (EVs) solely rely on electricity for propulsion, utilizing a large battery pack that needs to be recharged.</p></p>
