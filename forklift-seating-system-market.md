<p><p>The forklift seating system market comprises two main types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats are designed with mechanical springs or shock absorbers to provide shock isolation and vibration damping, ensuring a comfortable ride for forklift operators. On the other hand, air suspension seats use an air-filled bladder system that adjusts to the operator's weight and driving conditions, offering enhanced comfort and support. Both types aim at improving operator safety and reducing fatigue during long hours of forklift operation.</p></p>
