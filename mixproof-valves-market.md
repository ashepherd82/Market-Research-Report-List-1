<p><p>Mixproof valves are commonly used in industries that require the separation of different media, preventing any undesired mixing. Pneumatic mixproof valves employ compressed air to actuate the valve, granting control over the flow of media. On the other hand, hydraulic mixproof valves utilize fluid pressure to operate the valve. Both types offer reliable and efficient separation, with the choice between pneumatic and hydraulic depending on factors such as the required pressure rating, application complexity, and availability of air or fluid supply.</p></p>
