<p><p>The medicine packaging adhesive market can be divided into three main types: solvent adhesive, solvent-free adhesive, and waterborne adhesives. Solvent adhesive is a type of adhesive that contains solvents, which help in the bonding process but may release harmful emissions. On the other hand, solvent-free adhesive does not contain any solvents, making it environmentally friendly and safe for use. Waterborne adhesives are formulated with water as a carrier, making them non-toxic and easy to clean up. These different types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of the medicine packaging industry.</p></p>
