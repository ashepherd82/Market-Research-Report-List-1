<p><p>A cardiac rehabilitation device is a medical device that helps individuals who have suffered from cardiac events, such as heart attacks or heart surgeries, to recover and regain their physical strength. The market analysis indicates that this growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases worldwide is driving the demand for cardiac rehabilitation devices. The rising adoption of sedentary lifestyles, unhealthy eating habits, and lack of physical activity has led to a surge in cardiovascular diseases, thereby increasing the need for effective rehabilitation solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative cardiac rehabilitation devices, which are more efficient and user-friendly. These devices offer various features such as real-time monitoring, personalized exercise programs, and integration with mobile applications, which enhance patient engagement and compliance.</p><p>The market trend of integrating virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies into cardiac rehabilitation devices is gaining traction.
