<p><p>The ultra-capacitor market for electric buses can be categorized into three types: electrochemical double layer capacitors (EDLC), hybrid capacitors, and pseudo capacitors. Electrochemical double layer capacitors are known for their high capacitance and storage ability, while hybrid capacitors combine the features of EDLC and lithium-ion batteries. Pseudo capacitors have a higher energy density compared to EDLC but lower than hybrid capacitors. These different types cater to the diverse needs of the electric bus market, offering varying performance levels and capacities for energy storage.</p></p>
