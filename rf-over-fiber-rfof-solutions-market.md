<p><p>RF-over-Fiber (RFoF) solutions enable the transmission of radio frequency (RF) signals over optical fibers. The market for RFoF solutions includes various types of products. Standalone modules are individual units that convert RF signals to optical signals and vice versa. Subsystems consist of multiple interconnected modules. Chassis are modular platforms that can house multiple modules. Splitters and combiners allow the distribution and merging of optical signals. Optical switches enable the routing of optical signals. Optical DE multiplexers separate multiple optical signals into their respective channels. Other categories include additional products and solutions that support the RFoF technology.</p></p>
