<p><p>The CHNSO analyzer market encompasses three main types: GC chromatography, frontal chromatography, and adsorption-desorption. GC chromatography involves separating and analyzing the components of a gas mixture, while frontal chromatography involves analyzing the components of a liquid mixture as it flows through a solid stationary phase. Adsorption-desorption, on the other hand, refers to the attachment and subsequent release of molecules onto a solid surface, allowing for their analysis. These techniques are widely used in various industries for elemental analysis, organic compound detection, and material identification.</p></p>
