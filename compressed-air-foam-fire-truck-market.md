The primary factors driving this growth are the increasing awareness about the benefits of foam-based firefighting systems and the rise in fire incidents globally. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on developing lightweight and compact CAFFT models to enhance maneuverability and ease of operation.</p><p>The growing emphasis on environmental sustainability is also driving the market demand for CAFFTs. Foam-based firefighting systems have a lesser environmental impact compared to traditional water-based systems, as they require lesser water usage and produce less runoff.
