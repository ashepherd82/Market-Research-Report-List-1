<p><p>The airway/lung stent market is divided into different types based on the materials used. Metal stents are commonly made of stainless steel or nitinol and are known for their durability and ability to maintain airway patency. Silicone stents are made of flexible silicone material and are often used in pediatric cases due to their pliability. Hybrid stents combine metal and silicone materials to provide a balance between structural support and flexibility. Other types of stents may include bioabsorbable stents or drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent inflammation or restenosis.</p></p>
