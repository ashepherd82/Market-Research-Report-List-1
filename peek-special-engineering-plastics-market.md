<p><p>The PEEK Special Engineering Plastics market consists of three major types: PEEK-1000, PEEK-HPV, and PEEK-GF30. PEEK-1000 offers excellent dimensional stability, high chemical resistance, and low moisture absorption, making it suitable for applications like electrical connectors and seals. PEEK-HPV, on the other hand, provides enhanced wear resistance and reduced friction, making it ideal for gears and bearings. Lastly, PEEK-GF30 combines the properties of PEEK with 30% glass fiber reinforcement, resulting in improved strength, stiffness, and creep resistance, making it useful for structural components in industries such as automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
