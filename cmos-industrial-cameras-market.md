<p><strong>The CMOS Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS Industrial Cameras are broadly classified into two market types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area Scan Camera captures images in a grid-like pattern and is suitable for static or moving objects within the camera's field of view. It is used in applications such as inspection, measurements, and quality control. On the other hand, Line Scan Camera captures images line by line, making it ideal for inspecting continuous or moving objects, especially in high-speed production lines. This camera type is commonly used in industries like printing, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
