<p><p>Heat-exchange equipment is categorized into different types based on their design and applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of tubes surrounded by a shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer in various industrial processes. Plate heat exchangers use thin metal plates to transfer heat between two fluids, making them compact and suitable for applications where space is limited. Air-cooled heat exchangers use ambient air as the cooling medium, making them ideal for applications in remote areas or where water availability is limited. These equipment play a crucial role in various industries for effective heat transfer.</p></p>
