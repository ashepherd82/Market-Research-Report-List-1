<p><p>The marine trenchers service market consists of two main types: mechanical trenchers and jet trenchers. Mechanical trenchers are equipments that use rotating cutter wheels or chains to excavate and create trenches in the seabed. They are commonly used for shallow water operations and can handle various soil conditions. On the other hand, jet trenchers use high-pressure water jets to cut and remove sediments, making them ideal for deeper water trenches. These trenchers are used in offshore oil and gas projects, submarine cable installations, and other underwater construction activities.</p></p>
