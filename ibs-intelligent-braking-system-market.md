<p><p>The Intelligent Braking System (IBS) is a technologically advanced braking system used in vehicles. There are two types of IBS markets: the Two-Box System and the One-Box System. The Two-Box System consists of two separate units, one for the master cylinder and another for the brake booster, giving it more flexibility and versatility. On the other hand, the One-Box System combines both units into a single integrated system, offering cost-effectiveness and compactness. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle safety and braking performance with their intelligent features and capabilities.</p></p>
