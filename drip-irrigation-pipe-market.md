<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Drip irrigation pipe market is divided into two types: surface drip irrigation and subsurface drip irrigation. Surface drip irrigation involves installing pipes on the soil surface, where water is supplied to plants directly through drip emitters. On the other hand, subsurface drip irrigation involves burying the pipes beneath the soil surface, delivering water directly to plant roots. Both types help conserve water and improve crop efficiency by providing precise and controlled irrigation, minimizing water waste and maximizing plant uptake.</p></p>
