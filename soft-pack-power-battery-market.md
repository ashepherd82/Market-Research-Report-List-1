<p><p>Soft Pack Power Battery Market is divided into two major types: Lithium Phosphate Battery and Ternary Lithium Battery. A Lithium Phosphate Battery, which is also known as LiFePO4 battery, utilizes lithium iron phosphate as the cathode material. These batteries offer a longer lifespan, higher power density, and enhanced safety features. On the other hand, Ternary Lithium Batteries use a combination of nickel, cobalt, and manganese as the cathode material. These batteries are known for their higher energy density, lightweight design, and efficient performance. Both market types contribute to the advancement of portable power solutions with their unique characteristics.</p></p>
