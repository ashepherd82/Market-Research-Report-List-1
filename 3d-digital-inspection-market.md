<p><p>The 3D digital inspection market comprises three key types: machine vision, metrology, and non-destructive testing (NDT). Machine vision involves the use of cameras and image processing software to inspect and analyze products for defects or errors. Metrology focuses on precise measurement and dimensional analysis of objects using specialized equipment. NDT refers to testing methods that assess the integrity of components or materials without damaging them. All these technologies contribute to quality control and assurance processes in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
