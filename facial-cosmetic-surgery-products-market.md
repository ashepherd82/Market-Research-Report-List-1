<p><p>Facial cosmetic surgery products can be categorized into two main types: implants and injectables. Implants are prosthetic devices used to augment or reshape specific areas of the face, such as the chin, jaw, or cheekbones. They are often made of materials like silicone or porous polyethylene. Injectable products, on the other hand, refer to substances like botulinum toxin (Botox) or dermal fillers that are injected into the face to reduce wrinkles, restore volume, or enhance facial features. Both implants and injectables are popular choices for individuals seeking facial rejuvenation or enhancement.</p></p>
