<p><p>The magnetic sorter market is divided into three main types: electromagnetic magnetic sorter, permanent magnet magnetic sorter, and others. The electromagnetic magnetic sorter uses an electric current to generate a magnetic field, allowing it to sort materials based on their magnetic properties. On the other hand, the permanent magnet magnetic sorter utilizes a fixed magnetic field that is generated by permanent magnets. Lastly, the "others" category includes magnetic sorters that utilize alternative technologies or a combination of electromagnetic and permanent magnet systems to accomplish the sorting process.</p></p>
