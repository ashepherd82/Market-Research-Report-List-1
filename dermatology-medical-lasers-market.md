<p><p>Dermatology medical lasers encompass various types, including surgical lasers and vascular lasers. Surgical lasers are used for precise tissue cutting, vaporization, and coagulation during surgical procedures. They offer superior control and minimal damage to surrounding tissues. Vascular lasers are specifically designed to treat vascular skin conditions like spider veins, rosacea, and birthmarks. These lasers emit light that targets and destroys blood vessels, leading to their shrinkage or elimination. Vascular lasers are effective in improving the appearance of these conditions without causing significant damage to the skin.</p></p>
