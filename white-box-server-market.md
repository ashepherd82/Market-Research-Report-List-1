<p><p>The white box server market consists of three main types: rack-mount servers, blade servers, and whole cabinet servers. Rack-mount servers are typically mounted on server racks and are known for their compact size and high density. Blade servers are a modular design where multiple servers can be housed in a single enclosure, allowing for easy scalability and reduced cabling. Whole cabinet servers are larger units that can house multiple servers, storage devices, and networking equipment in a single cabinet, providing a comprehensive solution for data centers.</p></p>
