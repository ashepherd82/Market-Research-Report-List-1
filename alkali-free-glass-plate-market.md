<p><p>The alkali-free glass plate market can be categorized into two types based on the composition of the glass. The first type is alkali-free aluminosilicate glass, which is made by replacing the alkali content with aluminum ions in the glass matrix. This type of glass is known for its high chemical durability and resistance to thermal shock. The second type is alkali-free borosilicate glass, which replaces the alkali component with boron oxide. This glass offers excellent thermal resistance and low thermal expansion properties, making it suitable for various applications.</p></p>
