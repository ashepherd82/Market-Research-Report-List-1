Cutting involves the use of various cutting tools to shape the metal mask according to the required dimensions. Etching, on the other hand, utilizes chemical reactions to selectively remove material from the metal mask, enabling intricate pattern designs. These processes are crucial in the manufacturing of electronic devices and play a vital role in the CMM market.</p></p>
