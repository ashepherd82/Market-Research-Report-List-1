<p><p>The solar panel tracker market includes two main types: single-axis trackers and dual-axis trackers. Single-axis trackers move the solar panels along one axis, usually from east to west, to maximize sunlight exposure throughout the day. They are cost-effective and commonly used in utility-scale solar projects. On the other hand, dual-axis trackers move the panels along both horizontal and vertical axes, enabling them to track the sun's movement more accurately. This type is more expensive but provides higher energy yields and is suitable for smaller-scale installations or locations with irregular or lower solar irradiance.</p></p>
