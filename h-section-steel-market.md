<p><p>The H-section steel market consists of different types of steel beams, including hot-rolled H-beams and welded H-beams. Hot-rolled H-beams are manufactured through a process of heating steel billets to high temperatures and then shaping them into desired H-section profiles. These beams are known for their structural strength and wide range of applications in various industries. On the other hand, welded H-beams are produced by welding individual steel plates or sections together. These beams are preferred for their cost-effectiveness and flexibility in design, making them suitable for construction projects.</p></p>
