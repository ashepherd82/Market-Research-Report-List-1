<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Lens coating is used to enhance the durability, performance, and functionality of lenses. There are different types of lens coating technologies available in the market. Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD) utilizes ion beams to assist in the deposition process, improving adhesion and film quality. Electron Beam Evaporation employs an electron beam to evaporate materials and create a thin film coating on the lens surface. Sputtering involves bombarding a target material with high-energy particles, producing a thin film coating. Vacuum Deposition encompasses various techniques that involve depositing the desired material onto the lens surface using a vacuum chamber.</p></p>
