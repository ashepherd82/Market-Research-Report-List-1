<p><p>Tailings Storage Facilities (TSFs) are used to store waste materials generated from mining operations. The market for TSFs includes different types, such as Dry Stacking, Underground Storage, and Others. Dry Stacking involves stacking the tailings in a consolidated form on a dry site, minimizing water usage and preventing the formation of liquid tailings. Underground Storage refers to storing tailings in underground excavations, reducing surface area requirements. The "Others" category encompasses alternative storage methods, including in-pit deposition or the use of tailings dams. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations for tailings management in the mining industry.</p></p>
