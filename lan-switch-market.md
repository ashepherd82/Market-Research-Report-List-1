<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LAN Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switching</li><li>Layer 3 Switching</li><li>Layer 4 Switching</li><li>Multi-Layer switching (MLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LAN switch market is categorized into different types based on the switching layers they operate. Layer 2 switches operate at the data link layer and are responsible for organizing and directing data packets within a local network. Layer 3 switches function at the network layer, enabling routing and network address translation. Layer 4 switches operate at the transport layer, allowing advanced packet filtering and traffic monitoring. Multi-Layer switches combine features of layers 2, 3, and 4, providing enhanced performance and flexibility in managing network traffic.</p></p>
