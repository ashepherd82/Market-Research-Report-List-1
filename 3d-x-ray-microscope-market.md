<p><strong>The 3D X-ray Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D X-ray Microscope market is divided into two types: Transmission X-Ray Microscope (TXM) and Scanning X-Ray Microscope (SXM). TXM utilizes X-rays that pass through the sample to create an image, providing high-resolution details of the internal structure. On the other hand, SXM scans the sample with a focused X-ray beam to generate a 3D image at the surface, allowing for the visualization of features in the nanometer range. Both types of microscopes are used in various scientific and industrial applications, offering valuable insights into the microstructure of materials.</p></p>
