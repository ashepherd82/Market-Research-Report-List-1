<p><p>A microwave delay line is a device used to introduce delay in microwave signals. It consists of a waveguide or coaxial cable that is coiled up to form a delay structure. When a microwave signal is input into the delay line, it travels through the coiled path, resulting in a longer propagation time and therefore a delayed output signal. Microwave delay lines are commonly used in various applications such as radar systems, telecommunications, electronic warfare, and satellite communications.</p><p>The market analysis of the microwave delay line market indicates significant growth potential.
