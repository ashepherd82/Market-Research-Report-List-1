<p><p>The Automatic Infrared Motion Sensor Trash Can market is divided into three types based on the method of triggering the sensor. The Knee Induction trash can is operated by sensing the movement of the knee towards the sensor, allowing hands-free operation. The Hand Induction trash can detects the proximity of the hand to the sensor, enabling touchless disposal. The Foot Induction trash can is activated by the motion of the foot towards the sensor, providing a hygienic and convenient waste disposal solution. These types cater to different preferences and offer a convenient and sanitary way to dispose of trash.</p></p>
