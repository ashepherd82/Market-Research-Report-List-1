<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The powdered metal gear market is categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Spur gears are the simplest and most common type, featuring straight teeth that transmit motion in one direction. Helical gears have slanted teeth, offering smoother and quieter operation but producing axial forces. Bevel gears have conical teeth and allow the transmission of motion between non-parallel shafts. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses different gear types like worm gears, rack and pinion gears, and planetary gears, each having unique characteristics suited for specific applications.</p></p>
