<p><p>Laminar flow chambers are used in various industries to maintain a sterile environment for conducting research or manufacturing processes. They come in two main types - vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow chambers provide a downward flow of filtered air that is particularly useful when working with volatile substances. Horizontal laminar flow chambers, on the other hand, create a horizontal flow of filtered air, suitable for protecting samples and equipment. These different types cater to specific needs, ensuring cleanliness and safety in different environments.</p></p>
