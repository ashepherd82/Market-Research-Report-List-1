<p><p>The narrow finger wire duct market consists of two main types: slotted wire ducts and solid wall wire ducts. Slotted wire ducts have small finger-like slots along their length, allowing for easy insertion and removal of wires. These ducts are commonly used in applications where frequent wire changes are required. On the other hand, solid wall wire ducts have a smooth interior surface and provide better protection for wires, making them ideal for applications where wire stability and security are essential. Both types cater to different wire management needs in various industries.</p></p>
