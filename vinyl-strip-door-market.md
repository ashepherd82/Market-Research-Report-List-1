<p><p>The vinyl strip door market can be categorized into three types: transparent strip door, super transparent strip door, and opaque strip door. Transparent strip doors are made of clear vinyl material, allowing optimal visibility and easy passage for workers or vehicles. Super transparent strip doors have enhanced clarity and transparency, offering a higher level of visibility than the standard transparent strip doors. On the other hand, opaque strip doors are made of vinyl material that obstructs visibility, providing privacy and separation within work areas. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
