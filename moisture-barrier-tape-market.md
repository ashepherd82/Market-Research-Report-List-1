<p><p>The moisture barrier tape market consists of different types of tapes designed to provide protection against moisture and water intrusion. Polyethylene tape is made of a durable plastic material that forms an effective moisture barrier. Asphalt-impregnated tape is coated with asphalt to enhance its water resistance. Asphalt-coated kraft paper tape combines the strength of kraft paper with the waterproofing properties of asphalt. Foil scrim kraft tape features a layer of foil for added moisture protection. Paper-backed aluminium tape combines the strength of aluminium with the flexibility of paper to create an effective moisture barrier.</p></p>
