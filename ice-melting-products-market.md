<p><p>The ice melting products market can be categorized into two types: liquid ice melting products and solid ice melting products. Liquid ice melting products are typically in liquid form, such as brine or calcium chloride solution, and are sprayed or poured onto icy surfaces to melt the ice. On the other hand, solid ice melting products come in forms like pellets or granules, including substances like rock salt or magnesium chloride. These solid products are spread over icy surfaces to accelerate the melting process and provide traction.</p></p>
