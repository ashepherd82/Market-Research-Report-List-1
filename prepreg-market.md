<p><ul><li>Glass Fiber Prepreg</li><li>Carbon Fiber Prepreg</li><li>Aramid Fiber Prepreg</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepreg market can be categorized into three main types - glass fiber prepreg, carbon fiber prepreg, and aramid fiber prepreg. Glass fiber prepreg is composed of glass fibers impregnated with a resin matrix, offering excellent electrical insulation properties. Carbon fiber prepreg combines carbon fibers and resin, providing superior strength and stiffness, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Aramid fiber prepreg consists of aramid fibers impregnated with resin, providing exceptional resistance to impact, heat, and abrasion, hence well-suited for protective and aerospace applications. These materials are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
