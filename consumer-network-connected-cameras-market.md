<p><p>Consumer network connected cameras are classified into three main types: PTZ type, fixed type, and "other" market. PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras provide the flexibility to remotely control the camera's movement, allowing users to pan horizontally, tilt vertically, and zoom in/out. Fixed cameras, on the other hand, are stationary and provide a fixed field of view. The "other" market includes specialized cameras, such as doorbell cameras, outdoor cameras, dome cameras, and bullet cameras, which cater to specific consumer needs and preferences, offering versatile surveillance options.</p></p>
