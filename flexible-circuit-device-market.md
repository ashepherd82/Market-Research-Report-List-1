<p><ul><li>Single-Sided</li><li>Double-Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible circuit device market consists of two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided flexible circuits have conductive material on only one side, while the other side remains free of any electrical components. These circuits provide a simple and cost-effective solution for basic electronic devices. On the other hand, double-sided flexible circuits have conductive material on both sides, which allows for more complex circuit designs and increased functionality. These circuits are commonly used in more advanced electronic devices that require higher performance and greater design flexibility.</p></p>
