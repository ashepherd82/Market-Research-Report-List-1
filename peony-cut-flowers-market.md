They are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and are often used in floral arrangements for special occasions. On the other hand, Paeonia Lactiflora, or Herbaceous Peonies, have herbaceous stems and smaller flowers. They are more commonly used as garden flowers and are popular due to their wide range of colors and fragrance. Both varieties have a significant presence in the cut flower market.</p></p>
