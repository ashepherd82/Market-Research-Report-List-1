<p><p>The industrial spray humidifier market can be categorized into two types: stationary and portable. Stationary humidifiers are designed to provide a consistent and continuous humidification solution for large industrial spaces. They are typically fixed in a specific location and are suitable for applications where humidity control is required over an extended period of time. On the other hand, portable humidifiers are smaller in size and can be easily moved from one location to another. They are mainly used in smaller industrial spaces or for temporary humidification needs.</p></p>
