<p><p>Slope stabilization products are essential in preventing soil erosion and maintaining the stability of slopes. Geotextiles are permeable fabrics used to strengthen soil and control erosion on slopes. They provide stability by distributing forces and reinforcing the soil structure. Geocells are honeycomb-like structures made from plastic or concrete that are used to confine soil and other fill materials. They improve slope stability by preventing lateral movement of soil and enhancing its resistance. Other slope stabilization products may include erosion control blankets, gabions, and retaining walls, which provide additional support and protection against slope failure.</p></p>
