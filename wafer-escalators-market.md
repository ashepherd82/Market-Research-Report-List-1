<p><p>Wafer escalators, also known as wafer elevators, are equipment used in the semiconductor industry to transport wafers during the manufacturing process. There are three main types of wafer escalators in the market:</p><p>1. Notch Finder Elevator: This type of wafer escalator uses sensors to locate the notch or flat side of the wafer and correctly align it during the transfer process.</p><p>2. Batch slide transfer: This wafer escalator is designed to transport multiple wafers simultaneously in a controlled manner, allowing for efficient batch processing.</p><p>3. Flat Finder Elevator: Similar to the notch finder elevator, this type of wafer escalator uses sensors to identify the flat side of the wafer and ensure proper alignment during transfer.</p><p>These different types of wafer escalators cater to the various needs and requirements of semiconductor manufacturers for precise and efficient wafer transportation.</p></p>
