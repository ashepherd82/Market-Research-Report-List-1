<p><strong>The Optical-grade PMMA Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast PMMA Sheet</li><li>Extruded PMMA Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical-grade PMMA sheet market consists of two main types: cast PMMA sheet and extruded PMMA sheet. Cast PMMA sheet is made by pouring the liquid form of PMMA onto a glass surface and allowing it to harden. This method results in a higher quality sheet with better optical clarity. On the other hand, extruded PMMA sheet is produced by forcing molten PMMA through a die to create a solid sheet. Although slightly lower in quality, extruded PMMA sheet offers cost advantages and is widely used in various applications.</p></p>
