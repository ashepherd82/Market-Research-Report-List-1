<p><p>Clear epoxy molding compound refers to a type of material used in the manufacturing of various products. The market is divided into two types: solid clear epoxy molding compound and liquid clear epoxy molding compound. Solid clear epoxy molding compound is in a solid-state, typically in the form of granules or pellets, which are melted and then molded into the desired shape. On the other hand, liquid clear epoxy molding compound is in a liquid state and can be directly poured into a mold, which then hardens and solidifies. Both types offer different advantages and are used in different applications within the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
