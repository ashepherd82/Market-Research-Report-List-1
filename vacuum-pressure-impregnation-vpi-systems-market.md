<p><p>Vacuum Pressure Impregnation (VPI) Systems are utilized in various industries for the treatment of porous materials to improve their strength and durability. Dip Tank Impregnation Systems involve immersing the material in a tank filled with impregnating resin, allowing it to penetrate the pores. Vacuum Only Impregnation (VOI) Systems, on the contrary, employ a vacuum chamber to remove air from the material, followed by injecting the resin under pressure. Other market types may include variations or alternative methods of impregnation that are specific to certain industries or materials.</p></p>
