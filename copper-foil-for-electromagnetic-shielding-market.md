Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrolysis process, which results in a high-quality, smooth, and uniform foil. Rolled copper foil, on the other hand, is manufactured by rolling and annealing processes and is known for its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types of copper foil provide excellent shielding properties and are widely used in electronic devices and communication systems.</p></p>
