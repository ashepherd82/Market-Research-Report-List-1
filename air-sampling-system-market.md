<p><p>The air sampling system market consists of two main types: pipe detectors and smoke detectors. Pipe detectors use a network of pipes to collect air samples and detect the presence of gases or pollutants. Smoke detectors, on the other hand, are designed to sense the existence of smoke particles in the air. Both types of detectors are crucial in maintaining air quality and protecting against potential hazards. While pipe detectors focus on gases and pollutants, smoke detectors are specifically designed to detect smoke particles.</p></p>
