Cement grouting machines are used for injecting cement-based grouts into structures to fill voids or strengthen foundations. They are commonly used in construction and civil engineering projects. On the other hand, chemical grouting machines are designed to inject chemical grouts, such as urethane or epoxy, into gaps to seal leaks or stabilize soil. These machines find applications in various industries, including mining, tunneling, and infrastructure development.</p></p>
