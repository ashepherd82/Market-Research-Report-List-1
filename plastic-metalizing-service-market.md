<p><p>Plastic metalizing service market types include flame spraying, vacuum metalization, and others. Flame spraying involves melting metallic material using a flame and then spraying it onto the plastic surface to form a metal layer. Vacuum metalization, on the other hand, entails depositing a thin metallic layer onto the plastic surface through a vacuum process, creating a reflective or metallic appearance. Other market types involve different methods or techniques used for metalizing plastic, apart from flame spraying and vacuum metalization.</p></p>
