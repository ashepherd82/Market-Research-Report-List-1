<p><p>Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensors market consists of different types such as RF-Modulated Light Sources, Range Gated Imagers, and Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers. RF-Modulated Light Sources use radio frequency signals to modulate light intensity, allowing accurate distance measurement. Range Gated Imagers incorporate pulsed light sources and synchronized sensors to capture distance information within a specific range. Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers directly measure the time taken for light to travel to and from an object, providing precise distance calculations. These different types offer various approaches to ToF sensing, catering to different application requirements.</p></p>
