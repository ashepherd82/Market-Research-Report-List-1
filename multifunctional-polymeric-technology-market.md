<p><p>The Multifunctional Polymeric Technology market includes various types such as Physical Mixing, Particle Surface Functionalization, Hydrophobically Modified Ethoxylate Urethanes (HEUR), and Others. Physical mixing involves blending different polymers to create desired properties. Particle surface functionalization focuses on modifying the surface of particles to enhance their functionality. HEUR are polymeric materials designed to improve the rheological properties of coatings. The category of Others encompasses various technologies that don't fall under the aforementioned types but still offer multifunctional capabilities, such as block copolymers and polymer blends.</p></p>
