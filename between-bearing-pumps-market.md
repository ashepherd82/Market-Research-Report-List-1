<p><p>Between Bearing Pumps (BBP) are a type of centrifugal pumps that are used in various industrial applications. They have different market types, namely BB1, BB2, BB3, BB4, and BB5. BB1 pumps are single-stage, double-suction pumps primarily used for high capacities and low to medium heads. BB2 pumps are double-stage, single-suction pumps suitable for higher pressures. BB3 pumps are multistage, double-suction pumps designed for high capacities and high-pressure services. BB4 pumps are multistage, single-suction pumps used for low capacities and high pressures. Lastly, BB5 pumps are vertical, multistage pumps used for low flow and high-pressure applications.</p></p>
