<p><p>The organic virgin coconut oil market offers two types: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed. Cold-pressed coconut oil is extracted by pressing fresh coconut meat at low temperatures, preserving its nutrients and flavor. This method is deemed superior due to its natural extraction process. Conversely, expeller-pressed coconut oil is obtained by pressing dried coconut meat without the use of chemical solvents. While it still offers health benefits, it may lack the distinct taste and aroma of cold-pressed oil. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking organic and unrefined options for cooking, skincare, and other uses.</p></p>
