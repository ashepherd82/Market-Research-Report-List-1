<p><p>A Software and Source Code Escrow Service is a legal arrangement between three parties: the software developer, the software licensee, and a trusted third party, known as an escrow agent. In this arrangement, the source code of the software is deposited with the escrow agent, who releases it to the licensee if certain predetermined conditions are met, such as the bankruptcy or non-performance of the software developer.</p><p>The Software and Source Code Escrow Service Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of software escrow agreements by organizations for managing the risks associated with third-party software vendors. As software plays a critical role in the operations of businesses across various sectors, ensuring access to the source code becomes vital. This market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the key trends in this market is the emergence of cloud-based escrow solutions. As organizations are increasingly migrating their software systems to the cloud, there is a growing demand for escrow services that can accommodate cloud-based applications. Cloud-based escrow solutions provide secure and efficient access to source code stored in the cloud, enabling organizations to quickly recover and resume software operations in case of emergencies.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also witnessing the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies into escrow services. AI and ML algorithms can automate the process of escrow agreement management, reducing manual efforts and improving efficiency. These technologies can also enable proactive monitoring and alerting, ensuring continuous compliance with escrow agreements.</p><p>In conclusion, the Software and Source Code Escrow Service Market is expected to experience steady growth, driven by the increasing need for risk management and the adoption of cloud-based and AI-powered escrow solutions.</p></p>
