<p><p>Plant Nutritional Lipids are essential fatty acids derived from plant sources, known for their numerous health benefits. Phospholipids are a type of lipid that play a crucial role in building cell membranes and promoting healthy cell function. They are found in high amounts in foods such as soybeans and sunflower seeds. Sterols, on the other hand, are plant compounds that resemble cholesterol and have been linked to reducing cholesterol levels in the body. They are naturally present in various plant oils, including soybean and corn oil. Both phospholipids and sterols are valuable components of a balanced and nutritious diet.</p></p>
