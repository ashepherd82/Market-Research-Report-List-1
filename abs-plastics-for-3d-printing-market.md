<p><p>ABS plastics for 3D printing are used in two main market types: flame retardant and impact resistance. Flame retardant ABS plastics are formulated to resist ignition and slow down the spread of flames, making them suitable for applications in industries where fire safety is crucial. On the other hand, impact resistance ABS plastics are designed to withstand external forces and reduce the risk of deformation or breakage, making them ideal for applications where durability and strength are paramount. Both market types offer specific benefits and cater to different industry needs in the 3D printing world.</p></p>
