There are two main market types for DFOS: Single-mode and Multi-mode. Single-mode fibers have a smaller core size and transmit signals in a single beam, making them suitable for long-distance applications with high data rates. Multi-mode fibers have a larger core size and transmit signals through multiple beams, making them ideal for short-distance and lower data rate applications.</p></p>
