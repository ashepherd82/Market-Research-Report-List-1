<p><p>The tire material market can be categorized into various types. Elastomers are crucial for tire manufacturing as they provide flexibility and durability. Reinforcing fillers enhance the strength and wear resistance of tires. Plasticizers are used to improve the flexibility and processability of elastomers. Chemicals are essential for various tire production processes. Metal reinforcements, such as steel belts, are employed to enhance the tire's structural integrity. Textile reinforcements, like polyester or nylon, are used for reinforcement and stability. These materials play vital roles in ensuring high-performance and long-lasting tires.</p></p>
