<p><p>The stainless steel fiber market consists of three main types: flat stainless steel fiber, hooked stainless steel fiber, and undulated stainless steel fiber. Flat stainless steel fibers are thin and have a smooth surface. Hooked stainless steel fibers have small hooks or bends, providing good anchorage within materials. Undulated stainless steel fibers have a wavy or zigzag shape, enhancing the bonding and interlocking with other materials. Each type of stainless steel fiber serves different purposes in various applications such as concrete reinforcement, textile manufacturing, and composite materials.</p></p>
