<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) is a versatile thermoplastic polymer used in various industries. It is available in different market types: homopolymer, copolymer, and impact copolymer. Homopolymer PP is made from a single monomer, resulting in a high crystalline structure and excellent strength, making it suitable for applications that require rigidity and stability. Copolymer PP is made by incorporating another monomer, enhancing its flexibility and impact resistance, making it ideal for applications that need flexibility and durability. Impact copolymer PP is created with a combination of monomers, offering a balance of strength and impact resistance for applications requiring both properties.</p></p>
