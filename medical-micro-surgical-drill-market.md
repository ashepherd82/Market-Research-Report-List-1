<p><p>The medical microsurgical drill market is categorized into three types: pneumatic drills, electric drills, and battery-powered drills. Pneumatic drills are powered by air pressure, providing high-speed rotations for precision drilling. Electric drills utilize electricity to generate rotational movements, offering control and accuracy. Battery-powered drills are operated by rechargeable batteries, offering mobility and convenient usage. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of medical professionals, allowing them to perform microsurgical procedures effectively and efficiently.</p></p>
