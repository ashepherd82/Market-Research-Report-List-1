<p><p>The flexible graphite seal material market encompasses two main types: plain graphite type and stainless steel flexible graphite type. </p><p>Plain graphite type refers to seal materials made solely of graphite. These materials are highly flexible and possess excellent thermal conductivity, making them suitable for various sealing applications. They are often used in high-temperature environments that require effective heat transfer and chemical resistance.</p><p>On the other hand, stainless steel flexible graphite type seal materials combine graphite with stainless steel. This composition enhances the material's strength and durability, making it more suitable for applications that involve high pressures or mechanical stress. The addition of stainless steel also provides better resistance against corrosion and oxidation.</p></p>
