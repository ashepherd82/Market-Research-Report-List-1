<p><p>Public cloud infrastructure services refer to the provision of computing resources, such as servers and storage, by third-party cloud service providers over the internet. These services are hosted on-premises or by a third-party provider and offer increased control and security over the data and infrastructure.</p><p>Hybrid cloud infrastructure services incorporate both public and private cloud models.
