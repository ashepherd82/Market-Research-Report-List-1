Sheet form syntactic foams are thin and flexible, commonly used in applications that require insulation or buoyancy. Rod form foams are cylindrical in shape and often used for applications like core materials or underwater structural components. Blocks form foams are large, solid pieces typically utilized in automotive, aerospace, and marine industries for structural applications.</p></p>
