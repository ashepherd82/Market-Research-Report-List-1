<p><p>Machine vision lenses are optical devices used in industrial applications to capture images and provide visual feedback to machines. The market for machine vision lenses comprises various types, including C-Mount, CS-Mount, F-Mount, V-Mount, T2-Mount, and other lens mounts. C-Mount lenses have a 1-inch thread and are widely used due to their flexibility and compatibility. CS-Mount lenses are similar but have a shorter flange back distance. F-Mount lenses are popular for their compatibility with digital single-lens reflex cameras. V-Mount lenses are specifically designed for video cameras. T2-Mount lenses have a T-thread and are common in astronomy cameras. Other lens mounts cater to specific machine vision requirements.</p></p>
