<p><p>Polyanhydrides are a type of polymer that consist of repeating units of anhydride groups. Two common types of polyanhydrides are poly(sebacic anhydride) (PSAn) and poly(azelaic anhydride) (PAAn). PSAn is derived from sebacic acid and is known for its biodegradable and biocompatible properties. It is used in various biomedical applications such as drug delivery systems. PAAn, on the other hand, is synthesized from azelaic acid and is also biodegradable. It is primarily used in the development of controlled-release drug delivery systems due to its unique release kinetics.</p></p>
