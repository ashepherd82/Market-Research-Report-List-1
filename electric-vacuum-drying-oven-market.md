They can be classified into two main types: vertical and horizontal market. In the vertical market, the ovens are designed with vertically oriented chambers, allowing for easy loading and unloading of the samples from the top. On the other hand, in the horizontal market, the ovens have horizontally oriented chambers, suitable for larger and bulkier samples that require easy access from the front. Both types offer efficient and controlled drying processes for different applications.</p></p>
