<p><p>Smoke exhaust ventilators are devices used to remove smoke and harmful gases from a building during fire emergencies. There are two types of smoke exhaust ventilators: side suction and top suction. Side suction ventilators are installed on the side walls of a building and extract smoke horizontally. Top suction ventilators, on the other hand, are installed on the roof and extract smoke vertically. Both types play a crucial role in expelling smoke to provide a safe escape route for occupants and enable easier firefighting operations.</p></p>
