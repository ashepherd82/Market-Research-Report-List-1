<p><p>Rockwool roof insulation is available in various types, including blankets, boards, and others. Blanket insulation is flexible and commonly installed between roof rafters or in attic spaces. It provides excellent thermal and acoustic insulation. Board insulation, on the other hand, is more rigid and is typically used in flat or pitched roofing systems. It offers high compressive strength and is resistant to moisture. Other types of rockwool roof insulation may include loose-fill insulation or pre-formed structural panels. This diversified range of options allows for flexibility and customization in roof insulation applications.</p></p>
