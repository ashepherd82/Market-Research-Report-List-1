<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>RFID Tag Chips are divided into different market types based on their operating frequencies. HF (High-Frequency) RFID Chip operates at frequencies between 3 to 30 MHz and is commonly used for access control, payment systems, and asset tracking. UHF (Ultra-High Frequency) RFID Chip operates at frequencies between 850 to 950 MHz or 2.4 to 2.5 GHz and is suitable for supply chain management, inventory tracking, and automotive applications. LF (Low-Frequency) RFID Chip operates at frequencies between 30 to 300 KHz and is typically used for keyless entry systems, animal identification, and vehicle immobilization.</p></p>
