<p><p>Elastic gaskets are flexible components used to provide a seal between two or more surfaces to prevent leakage or contamination. There are different types of elastic gaskets available in the market. Saddle gaskets are shaped like a saddle and provide an efficient seal by conforming to the surface irregularities. Waveform gaskets have a wavy or corrugated shape, allowing them to effectively seal against uneven or dynamic surfaces. Both saddle and waveform gaskets offer versatile sealing solutions for various industrial applications with their ability to adapt to different surface conditions.</p></p>
