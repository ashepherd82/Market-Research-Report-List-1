<p><ul><li>Ilmenite</li><li>Titanium Slag</li><li>Rutile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium ore market encompasses three main types: ilmenite, titanium slag, and rutile. Ilmenite is the most abundant and widely used ore, containing titanium and iron. Titanium slag is a byproduct of ilmenite processing and is rich in titanium dioxide. It is often used in the production of titanium metal. Rutile is a high-grade titanium ore, typically used in the production of titanium dioxide pigment. These market types cater to different industries and applications, contributing to the overall demand and dynamics of the titanium ore market.</p></p>
