<p><p>A Gas-lined reactor, also known as a gas-cooled reactor or GCR, is a type of nuclear reactor that uses carbon dioxide as a coolant. This type of reactor offers several advantages, including high-temperature operation, efficient heat transfer, and enhanced safety features.</p><p>The market outlook for gas-lined reactors is promising, with a projected CAGR of 10.8% during the forecasted period.
