<p><p>The vehicle suspension market consists of two main types: hydraulically actuated suspension and electronically actuated suspension. </p><p>Hydraulically actuated suspension systems use hydraulic fluid to adjust the vehicle's height and absorb shocks from the road. These systems provide a smooth and comfortable ride by maintaining a consistent vehicle height and reducing the impact of bumps and potholes.</p><p>Electronically actuated suspension systems, on the other hand, utilize electronic sensors and control units to adjust the suspension in real-time. This allows for more precise and customizable suspension settings, enhancing the vehicle's handling and overall performance.</p></p>
