Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to create catalysts that can offer better performance and durability, while also meeting stricter emission standards.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a shift towards selective catalytic reduction (SCR) technology, which utilizes a urea-based solution (AdBlue) to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water.
