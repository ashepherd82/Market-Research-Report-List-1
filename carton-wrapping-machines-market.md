<p><p>Carton wrapping machines are used to securely package products in cartons for transportation and storage. They come in three different market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require human intervention for each step of the wrapping process, making them suitable for smaller scale operations. Semi-automatic machines automate certain steps, such as sealing or cutting, reducing human effort while maintaining some level of manual control. Automatic machines fully automate the entire wrapping process, increasing efficiency and requiring minimal human involvement. These market types cater to different production scales and cost considerations.</p></p>
