<p><p>Boom sprayers are widely used in agricultural fields for efficient and precise application of pesticides, fertilizers, and herbicides. The market is segmented into three main types: horizontal boom type, derrick boom type, and air bag type. Horizontal boom sprayers are mounted on the rear of agricultural vehicles and provide a wide coverage area. Derrick boom sprayers use a derrick boom structure to control the spraying height and direction. Air bag boom sprayers utilize air bags to support the boom, ensuring stability and reducing soil compaction during spraying operations.</p></p>
