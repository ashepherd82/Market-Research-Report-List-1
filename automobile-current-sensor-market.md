<p><p>The automobile current sensor market is comprised of various types of sensors used for measuring current in vehicles. Hall-Effect current sensors utilize the Hall Effect phenomenon to determine current levels. AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance) current sensors measure current through changes in magnetic resistance. GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance) current sensors utilize the GMR effect for current measurement. TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) current sensors measure current based on tunneling magnetoresistance. Additionally, there are other types of current sensors available in the market, each utilizing different principles for current measurement in automobiles.</p></p>
