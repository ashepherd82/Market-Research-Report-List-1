<p><strong>The LNG Loading & Offloading Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CBM (Conventional Buoy Mooring)</li><li>SPM (Single Point Mooring)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG Loading & Offloading Systems encompass different types of technologies used for the transfer of liquefied natural gas (LNG) between carriers and onshore terminals. CBM is a conventional buoy mooring system that utilizes a fixed buoy to connect the LNG carrier to the terminal, offering a reliable and cost-effective solution. On the other hand, SPM, or Single Point Mooring, employs a single buoy connected to the carrier via a floating hose system, allowing for flexible low-drag mooring. Both systems play a crucial role in the safe and efficient transfer of LNG.</p></p>
