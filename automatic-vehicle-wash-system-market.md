<p><p>The automatic vehicle wash system market is divided into two types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. On the other hand, fully-automatic systems do not require any manual intervention and operate with minimal human involvement. These systems are more advanced, efficient, and costly. They usually offer a range of automated functions, including water spraying, soap application, brushing, and drying.</p></p>
