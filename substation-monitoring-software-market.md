<p><p>Substation monitoring software market is categorized into three main types: asset management, production management, and performance management. Asset management software helps in managing and preserving substation assets, such as transformers and circuit breakers, by monitoring their condition and scheduling maintenance. Production management software optimizes substation operations by monitoring real-time data like voltage and current, ensuring smooth energy production. Performance management software analyzes historical data and identifies improvement areas to enhance overall substation performance. These software types enable efficient monitoring and control of substation operations, ensuring reliability and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
