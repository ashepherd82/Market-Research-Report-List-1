<p><p>The market for Parallel Micro Grippers is divided into two main types: Pneumatic Parallel Micro Grippers and Electric Parallel Micro Grippers. Pneumatic Parallel Micro Grippers utilize compressed air to operate, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution for gripping small objects. On the other hand, Electric Parallel Micro Grippers utilize electric motors for precise and controlled gripping. They offer higher precision and flexibility but are usually more expensive than their pneumatic counterparts. Both types of grippers find applications in industries like electronics, pharmaceuticals, and research, where precise gripping of small objects is required.</p></p>
