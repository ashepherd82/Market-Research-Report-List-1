<p><p>The wall oven market consists of three main types: single wall ovens, double wall ovens, and combined wall ovens. A single wall oven is a stand-alone unit with one cooking chamber, ideal for smaller kitchens or individuals with less cooking needs. On the other hand, a double wall oven features two separate cooking chambers, allowing for simultaneous cooking or different temperature settings. Lastly, a combined wall oven merges a regular wall oven with a microwave or other cooking appliances, providing versatility and convenience in one unit.</p></p>
