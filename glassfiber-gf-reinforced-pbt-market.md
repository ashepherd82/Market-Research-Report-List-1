It is commonly used in applications such as automotive parts, electrical enclosures, and consumer goods.</p><p>On the other hand, Short Fiber GF Reinforced PBT is made by blending short glass fibers with PBT resin. This type offers improved dimensional stability, toughness, and electrical insulation properties. It finds application in sectors like electronics, telecommunications, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
