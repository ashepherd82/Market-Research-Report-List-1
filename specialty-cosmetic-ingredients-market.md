<p><p>The Specialty Cosmetic Ingredients Market comprises various types of ingredients that play different roles in cosmetic products. Emollients are moisturizing agents that help soften and smooth the skin. Surfactants are cleansing agents that create lather and remove dirt and oil. Anionics are negatively charged ingredients that improve foam stability. Non-anionics are similar to anionics but lack a negative charge. Cationics are positively charged ingredients that help condition the hair. Amphoterics can act as either a surfactant or conditioning agent. Other specialty cosmetic ingredients include preservatives, antioxidants, fragrances, and colorants.</p></p>
