<p><p>A Hard Disc Drive (HDD) is a non-volatile computer storage device that uses magnetic storage to store and retrieve digital data. <p><p>The hard disc drive (HDD) market consists of three main types: portable HDDs, desktop HDDs, and network-attached storage (NAS) HDDs. Desktop HDDs are larger in size and offer higher storage capacities, primarily used for personal computers.
