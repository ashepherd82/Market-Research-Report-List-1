<p><p>The Biliary Stent Systems Market consists of two main types - Conventional Un-covered Stent and Hybrid weaving type Stent. A Conventional Un-covered Stent is a tube-like device made of metal or plastic that is used to open up blocked bile ducts and allow the proper flow of bile. On the other hand, a Hybrid weaving type Stent combines the advantages of both covered and uncovered stents. It is designed to prevent tumor and tissue ingrowth, while also maintaining the required flexibility. Both types are used to treat bile duct obstructions and restore normal bile flow.</p></p>
