<p><p>The conductive elastomer gasket market is segmented into three types: conductive silicone, conductive rubber, and others. Conductive silicone gaskets are made from silicone material that has conductive properties, allowing them to provide electrical conductivity and shielding against electromagnetic interference. Conductive rubber gaskets, on the other hand, are made from rubber material with conductive fillers, ensuring electrical conductivity and providing effective sealing. The "others" category includes conductive elastomer gaskets made from materials other than silicone or rubber, such as fluorosilicone or conductive polymers.</p></p>
