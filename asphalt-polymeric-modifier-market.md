<p><p>The Asphalt Polymeric Modifier market consists of two types: Styrene-butadiene-styrene (SBS) and Ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA). SBS is a synthetic elastomer that enhances the properties of asphalt, including improved flexibility, durability, and resistance to cracking. It is widely used in road construction. On the other hand, EVA is a thermoplastic copolymer that provides additional benefits like improved bond strength and increased resistance to deformation. It is commonly used in pavement preservation applications to increase the lifespan of asphalt surfaces. Both SBS and EVA contribute to enhancing the performance and longevity of asphalt.</p></p>
