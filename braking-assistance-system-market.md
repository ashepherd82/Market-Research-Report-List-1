<p><p>Braking Assistance System (BAS) market consists of two types: Type I and Type II. Type I BAS involves basic features like anti-lock braking system (ABS) and electronic brake-force distribution (EBD). It aids in preventing wheel lockup during sudden braking, enhancing control and stability. On the other hand, Type II BAS offers advanced features such as autonomous emergency braking (AEB) and forward collision warning (FCW). Type II systems provide additional safety by applying brakes automatically when a potential collision is detected, offering increased protection to drivers and pedestrians.</p></p>
