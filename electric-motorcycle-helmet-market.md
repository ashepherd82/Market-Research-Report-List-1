<p><p>The electric motorcycle helmet market comprises two main types: Full Face Helmets and Open Face Helmets. Full Face Helmets provide all-around protection for the rider's head, covering the entire face, including the chin. They are suitable for high-speed riding and offer better safety features. On the other hand, Open Face Helmets cover the top and back of the head but leave the face exposed. These helmets are more comfortable and allow for better visibility but offer less protection compared to Full Face Helmets.</p></p>
