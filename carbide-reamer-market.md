<p><p>Carbide reamers are cutting tools used for enlarging or finishing holes in various materials. Monobloc type reamers are made from a single piece of carbide and offer high rigidity and stability, making them suitable for precision machining. Insert type reamers, on the other hand, consist of a carbide body with replaceable inserts. These inserts can be easily changed, reducing the cost of regrinding the entire tool. This type of reamer is versatile and provides good cutting performance. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
