Centre Wheel Drive Electric Wheelchairs have a drive wheel located beneath the user's body, providing better maneuverability and turning capabilities. Front Wheel Drive Electric Wheelchairs have their drive wheel positioned in the front, offering better traction and stability on uneven surfaces. Standing Electric Wheelchairs enable users to transition from a seated to a standing position, enhancing independence and promoting better posture. These powerchairs cater to the specific needs of users with limited mobility within indoor settings.</p></p>
