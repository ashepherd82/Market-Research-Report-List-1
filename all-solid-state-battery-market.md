On the other hand, all-solid-state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes use solid inorganic materials as the electrolyte, offering higher energy density and stability, making them suitable for larger-scale applications. Both types aim to replace traditional liquid-based batteries with safer and more efficient alternatives.</p></p>
