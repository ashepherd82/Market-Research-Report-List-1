<p><ul><li>Welded Tube</li><li>Seamless Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel tubes market consists of two types: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are made by joining two pieces of steel together through welding, creating a strong and durable tube. These tubes are commonly used in industries such as automotive and construction. On the other hand, seamless tubes are formed from a single piece of steel, without any welded seams. They offer higher strength and are often preferred in industries such as oil and gas, where reliability and integrity are crucial.</p></p>
