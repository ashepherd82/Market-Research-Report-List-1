<p><p>The Electroencephalogram (EEG) Devices Market consists of three types of equipment: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG devices are the traditional ones used to record brain activity through electrodes on the scalp. Video EEG devices combine the conventional EEG with video recording capabilities to capture and analyze brain activity alongside corresponding video data. Dynamic EEG devices provide real-time monitoring of brain activity during specific activities, such as sleep or while performing tasks, enabling a more comprehensive analysis of brain function.</p></p>
