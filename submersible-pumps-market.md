<p><ul><li>Borewell Submersible Pump</li><li>Open Well Submersible Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submersible pumps are categorized into different types based on their applications. Borewell submersible pumps are specifically designed to be used in deep wells and boreholes, where they are submerged in water, enabling them to pump water efficiently from underground sources. On the other hand, open well submersible pumps are used in open wells or tanks, where they are also submerged to pump water. These pumps are suitable for shallow water sources. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the reliable supply of water for various residential, industrial, and agricultural needs.</p></p>
