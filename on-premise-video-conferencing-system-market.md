<p><ul><li>Multipoint Systems</li><li>Endpoint Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-premise video conferencing system market consists of two types: multipoint systems and endpoint systems. </p><p>Multipoint systems enable simultaneous communication between three or more participants in different locations, facilitating real-time collaboration. These systems typically require a central server to manage the conference and distribute the audio and video streams to all participants.</p><p>Endpoint systems, on the other hand, are individual video conferencing devices that are set up in dedicated meeting rooms or personal workstations. They connect directly to other video conferencing endpoints or multipoint systems to enable high-quality video communication.</p></p>
