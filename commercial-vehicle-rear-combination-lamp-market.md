<p><p>The commercial vehicle rear combination lamp market comprises two main types: Bulb Type and LED Type. Bulb Type lamps utilize traditional incandescent bulbs, which produce light by passing an electric current through a wire filament. On the other hand, LED Type lamps use Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) to generate light. LEDs are more energy-efficient, durable, and offer better visibility, but they can be costlier than bulb-type lamps. Both types of lamps play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and visibility of commercial vehicles on the road.</p></p>
