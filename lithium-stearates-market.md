<p><p>Lithium stearates are widely used in various industries, with two main types being stearic acid lithium stearate and lithium hydroxy stearate. Stearic acid lithium stearate is derived from the reaction between stearic acid and lithium hydroxide, and it exhibits excellent stabilizing and thickening properties. On the other hand, lithium hydroxy stearate is produced by reacting lithium hydroxide with hydroxy stearic acid. This type of lithium stearate is commonly used as a lubricating agent due to its low melting point and good thermal stability.</p></p>
