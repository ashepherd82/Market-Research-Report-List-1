<p><p>Gas meters are devices used to measure the flow of gas. Wet gas meters are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. They operate by passing gas through a chamber filled with liquid to separate impurities before measurement. Dry gas meters, on the other hand, are used in industrial applications. They use rotating or oscillating mechanisms to measure gas flow without the need for a liquid chamber. Both types of gas meters play a crucial role in accurately measuring gas consumption for billing purposes, ensuring fair and efficient gas distribution systems.</p></p>
