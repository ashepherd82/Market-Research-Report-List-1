<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Underfill materials are used in the semiconductor industry to enhance the reliability and durability of electronic devices. There are three types of underfill materials: Capillary Underfill Material (CUF), No Flow Underfill Material (NUF), and Molded Underfill Material (MUF). CUF flows into the tiny gaps between the semiconductor chip and the substrate via capillary action. NUF does not flow but fills the gaps through a curing process. MUF is pre-molded and then placed between the chip and substrate, providing mechanical support. These materials help prevent cracking and enhance the electrical performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
