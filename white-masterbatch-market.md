<p><p>White masterbatch is a coloring additive used in plastic manufacturing. Type I white masterbatch is made from highly concentrated titanium dioxide pigment, offering excellent whiteness and opacity. It is preferred for applications where maximum whiteness is required, like in food packaging. Type II white masterbatch, on the other hand, is a blend of titanium dioxide with other additives to enhance characteristics like dispersion, heat stability, and UV protection. It is commonly used for general purposes where a high level of whiteness is not essential, such as in household goods and industrial products.</p></p>
