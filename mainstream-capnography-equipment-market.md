<p><p>Mainstream capnography equipment refers to the medical devices used to measure carbon dioxide levels in a patient's exhaled breath during surgical procedures and in critical care settings. Unlike sidestream capnography, which uses a sampling line to divert a small portion of exhaled breath, mainstream capnography equipment is directly connected to the patient's airway, providing real-time and continuous monitoring.</p><p>The future outlook for the mainstream capnography equipment market is promising. The increasing adoption of capnography monitoring in various healthcare facilities is driving market growth. Capnography offers several benefits, including early detection of respiratory compromise, improved patient safety, and enhanced monitoring during anesthesia administration. Moreover, the rising prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma, is also contributing to the market's expansion.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in mainstream capnography equipment are expected to further drive market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on developing compact and portable devices with advanced features like wireless connectivity and data analysis capabilities.
