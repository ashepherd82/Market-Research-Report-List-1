<p><p>The SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) is an essential tool used in the semiconductor market. FE-SEM (Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscope) and FIB-SEM (Focused Ion Beam Scanning Electron Microscope) are two types of SEMs widely used in this industry. FE-SEM uses a field emission source to generate high-resolution images and is particularly suitable for imaging conductive or semi-conductive materials. On the other hand, FIB-SEM combines a focused ion beam with SEM to enable precise milling, cutting, and imaging of samples, making it ideal for tasks such as circuit editing or failure analysis in the semiconductor market.</p></p>

SEM also finds utility in other areas like defect inspection, failure analysis, and lithography process optimization in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
