<p><p>The Signal Data Cables Market comprises three main types: Twisted Pair (Copper), Coax (Copper), and Optic Cables (Fiber). Twisted pair cables consist of twisted copper wires that provide reliable data transmission at shorter distances. Coaxial cables use copper conductors within layers of insulating material and shielding for better quality over longer distances. Optic cables use fiber-optic technology, consisting of thin glass fibers that transmit data as light signals, enabling high-speed and long-distance communication. These three types cater to various needs in the market, offering options for different distance requirements and data transmission speeds.</p></p>
