<p><p>Vanillin extract is available in various market types such as ferulic acid, eugenol, and coumarin pods. Ferulic acid is a compound found in plant cell walls and is used in the production of vanillin extract due to its aromatic properties. Eugenol, on the other hand, is a component of essential oils extracted from certain plants like cloves and provides a distinct flavor to vanillin extract. Lastly, coumarin pods are used as a natural source of vanillin and are known for their sweet and hay-like aroma.</p></p>
