<p><ul><li>Single Deck</li><li>Double Deck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear Motion Screens are used in various industries for reliable and efficient screening of materials. Single Deck Screens are designed with a single screen layer and are ideal for applications requiring finer particle separation. They are commonly used in mining, construction, and chemical industries. Double Deck Screens, on the other hand, consist of two screen layers placed one above the other. They are suitable for applications requiring multiple particle size classifications and can be found in mining, aggregate, and recycling industries. Both types play a crucial role in the screening process by effectively separating and sizing materials.</p></p>
