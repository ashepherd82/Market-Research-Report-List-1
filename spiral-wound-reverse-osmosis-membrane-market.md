<p><p>The spiral-wound reverse osmosis membrane market consists of two main types: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made up of multiple layers, with an active layer responsible for the separation process. These membranes offer high rejection rates and are widely used in water treatment applications. On the other hand, asymmetric membranes have a gradient structure with a thin active layer on top. They provide high permeability and are suitable for desalination processes. Both types play crucial roles in the reverse osmosis membrane market, addressing different needs in water purification and desalination industries.</p></p>
