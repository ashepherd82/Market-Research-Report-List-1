<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Starter Market consists of two main types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters use a direct current (DC) motor to engage the engine's flywheel directly. This type is commonly found in older vehicles. On the other hand, Electromagnetic Control starters rely on an electromagnetic solenoid to engage the flywheel. They are more commonly used in modern vehicles and provide faster and smoother engine start-up. Both types play a crucial role in initiating the engine's combustion process and ensuring the vehicle starts smoothly.</p></p>
