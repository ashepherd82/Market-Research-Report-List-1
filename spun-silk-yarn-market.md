<p><ul><li>Ring Spun</li><li>Open End/OE</li><li>Polyester Yarn</li><li>Semi-Worsted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spun silk yarn market comprises different types of yarn based on the spinning techniques used. Ring spun yarn is made by twisting and thinning the silk fibers to create a smooth and strong yarn. Open end or OE yarn is produced by spinning the fibers using a rotor, resulting in a coarser and less expensive yarn. Polyester yarn, on the other hand, is made from synthetic fibers and offers enhanced durability and resistance. Semi-worsted yarn involves a specific spinning process that produces a yarn with a slightly irregular texture. These different market types cater to varying customer preferences and usage requirements.</p></p>
