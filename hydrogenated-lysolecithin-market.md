<p><p>The hydrogenated lysolecithin market can be categorized into two types: fluid hydrolyzed and deoiled hydrolyzed. Fluid hydrolyzed hydrogenated lysolecithin is a type in which the hydrolysis process is carried out using fluid technology. This method ensures the conversion of lecithin into lysolecithin while maintaining its fluid state. Deoiled hydrolyzed hydrogenated lysolecithin, on the other hand, is produced by removing the oil content from hydrolyzed hydrogenated lecithin. Both types are used in various industries for their emulsification, foaming, and thickening properties.</p></p>
