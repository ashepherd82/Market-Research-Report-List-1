<p><p>The aluminum target market consists of two types: plane target and rotating target. A plane target is a flat surface made of aluminum that provides a stable platform for various applications, such as deposition of thin films. It is widely used in industries like semiconductors and optical coatings. On the other hand, a rotating target involves a cylindrical piece of aluminum that rotates during the deposition process. This type is commonly employed in industries such as solar cells and magnetic storage devices due to its ability to achieve uniform film coatings. Both types cater to different needs within the aluminum target market.</p></p>
