<p><p>The rotation resistant wire rope market includes two types: square section and hexagonal cross section. Square section wire ropes are characterized by their square-shaped cross section, providing enhanced resistance to rotation. These ropes are commonly used in applications where rotation resistance and stability are crucial, such as excavators and cranes. On the other hand, hexagonal cross section wire ropes have a hexagonal-shaped cross section, offering increased rotational stability and preventing kinking. These wire ropes are commonly used in applications where smooth and controlled rotation is essential, such as suspension bridges and oil rigs.</p></p>
