<p><p>The bulk chemical drum market comprises three main types: steel drums, fiber drums, and plastic drums. Steel drums are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for storing corrosive chemicals. Fiber drums, made from compressed layers of paperboard, offer lightweight and cost-effective packaging for non-hazardous materials. Plastic drums, made from high-density polyethylene, are versatile and commonly used for a wide range of chemicals due to their resistance to corrosion and leakage. Each type of drum caters to different chemical storage needs, ensuring safe and efficient handling.</p></p>
