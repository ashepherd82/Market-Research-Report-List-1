<p><p>Packaging printing market consists of various types including flexography printing, rotogravure printing, offset printing, digital printing, and screen printing. Flexography printing involves a flexible relief plate and is commonly used for printing on materials like plastic and paper bags. Rotogravure printing uses a cylindrical printing plate and is suitable for high-volume production of materials like labels and packaging films. Offset printing employs plates, rubber blankets, and ink rollers to transfer an image onto a substrate. Digital printing is a modern method that directly prints digital files onto packaging materials. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a stencil onto packaging materials and is often used for special effects and textured surfaces.</p></p>
