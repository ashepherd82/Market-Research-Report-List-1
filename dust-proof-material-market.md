<p><p>Dust-proof materials are designed to prevent the entry of dust particles into various applications. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is highly effective due to its low-friction surface and resistance to dust build-up. Aramid is renowned for its strength and heat resistance, making it suitable for dust-proof applications in harsh environments. Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) offers exceptional chemical resistance and thermal stability, ensuring effective dust protection.</p></p>
