<p><p>Drum brakes are a type of braking system used in vehicles. There are different types of drum brakes available in the market. The leading trailing shoe brake has two shoes, with one being smaller than the other. The smaller shoe leads while the larger shoe trails during braking. The two leading shoe brake has two equal-sized shoes that lead during braking. The servo brake uses a hydraulic system to boost the braking force and improve effectiveness. These variations in design and functionality provide options for manufacturers and customers to choose from in the drum brake market.</p></p>
