<p><p>Vibration level switches are used to detect and monitor the level of materials in tanks or containers. There are two main types: vibrating fork and vibrating rod. Vibrating fork switches consist of a tuning fork that vibrates at a certain frequency and when the material touches the fork, the vibration changes, indicating the presence or absence of material. Vibrating rod switches work similarly, but use a vibrating rod instead. Both switches are reliable and commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and food processing.</p></p>
