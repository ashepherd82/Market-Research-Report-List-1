<p><p>Seatbelt sensors market types include two-point safety belt, three-point safety belt, and four-point safety belt. A two-point safety belt consists of a lap belt that secures the occupant at two points across the waist. A three-point safety belt adds an additional shoulder strap to the lap belt, offering better protection. A four-point safety belt further enhances safety by adding a second shoulder strap, providing even more secure restraint. These different types of seatbelt sensors cater to varying levels of safety needs in vehicles, ensuring occupant protection in case of accidents.</p></p>
